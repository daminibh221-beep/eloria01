Our Story

A Legacy of
Craftsmanship

Born in the heart of Florence, Italy, Éloria represents the pinnacle of leather artistry — where centuries-old tradition meets contemporary design.

Every Éloria piece is meticulously handcrafted by our master artisans using only the finest full-grain Italian leather. From the careful selection of raw materials in Tuscan tanneries to the final hand-stitched detail, each bag undergoes over 120 individual steps to ensure unparalleled quality.

We believe that a handbag is more than an accessory — it is an extension of the woman who carries it. Bold, refined, and unapologetically elegant. That is the Éloria promise.
